Xische Reports is an independent investigative research publisher, made in Dubai.

We examine change across culture, innovation and the future to publish long-form, visual and interactive insights.

Our strategy firm Xische & Co has been helping governments, global brands and startups research, innovate, and communicate since 2001. Working with leaders across the public and private sector affords us deep analytical insight as a byproduct.

Our research and editorial teams convert this experience into tangible, long-form, visual and interactive reports, open to the general public, free of charge.

We publish in 3 ways.

1. state of play

A long-form report format tackling sector-specific change from a global or regional, big-picture, analytical lens. We currently publish the Smart Cities, Blockchain, Artificial Intelligence and Space Culture reports in this format.

2. navigator

An interactive visual platform that presents data to analyze future impact as UAE approaches its jubilee year in 2021. We currently publish the Navigator: UAE Strategy and Navigator: Future Cities in this format.

2. projects

We help clients build global thought -leadership by commissioned research and analysis in the form of articles, reports, digital platforms and events. We currently work with UAE Prime Minister`s Office, Smart Dubai, Dubai Municipality and the World Government Summit.

Sponsorships open.

As an independent publisher of multi-format research, Xische Reports offers 3 tiered sponsorship packages designed for our 3 products audience groups, across single or multiple reports. We currently work with partners including Smart Dubai, DEWA, DIFC Fintech Hive and World Blockchain Forum
